# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Pokemon players who want a clear team-building assistant across vanilla games, competitive formats, Radical Red, and Pokemon Champions. They may be planning casually, tuning a difficult run, or comparing competitive options, and they need recommendations that explain why a team works instead of only listing picks.

## Product Purpose

Equinox helps users build balanced Pokemon teams from an initial core, chosen format, and preferred team identity. Success means the user can enter a small set of Pokemon, understand the recommended additions, compare alternatives, and leave with a confident battle plan grounded in synergy, coverage, threats, speed, roles, and format-specific constraints.

## Brand Personality

Equilibrio, sinergia, direcionamento. The product should feel modern, humanized, calm, and precise: a guide that organizes complexity without making the user feel overwhelmed.

## Anti-references

Avoid noisy fan-site visuals, overloaded dashboards, excessive decorative color, childish game theming, generic SaaS gloss, and UI that feels more like a marketing landing page than a useful builder. Avoid clutter, visual imbalance, and effects that compete with the analysis.

## Design Principles

- Express balance everywhere: hierarchy, spacing, contrast, and component weight should feel composed rather than loud.
- Make synergy understandable: recommendations must expose the strategic reason behind each option in direct language.
- Guide without overwhelming: dense analysis is allowed, but the interface should stage information progressively and keep the primary action obvious.
- Keep the identity restrained: black, white, gray, and their variations should carry the system, with contrast and state used deliberately.
- Treat the user as a strategist: tone should be helpful and confident, not playful for its own sake.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Aim for a clean, readable, keyboard-friendly product interface. Preserve strong text contrast, visible focus states, reduced-motion alternatives for animation, and layouts that remain usable in Portuguese and English without text overflow.
